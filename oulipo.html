  • License: GPL v3

  • Fork me on github
  • Support, comments, requests over at GetSatisfaction

Inspired by and named after the group Oulipo, this clean, minimal, grid-based theme was created for authors who want their content to shine. Three-column simplicity with an eye for typography, Oulipo is also available on WordPress.com

Features

  1. Custom menu support
  2. Custom background and headers
  3. Post thumbnails
  4. A widget-enabled sidebar
  5. Front page styling for sticky posts
  6. Threaded comments for WordPress 2.7+
