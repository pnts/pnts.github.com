Andrea an NYC-based designer, beer-lover, and front-end nerd. She is currently the Chief Design Officer at SimpleReach, a startup providing content analytics to the world’s top publishers. Prior to SimpleReach, Andrea worked on mobile, tablet, and desktop applications for clients including AOL, MoMA, Emily Carr University, SocialFlow, Work Market, and LinkedIn. She was a local leader for both the Vancouver and New York branches of the Interaction Design Association, and was the Creative Director of NabeWise, a neighborhood-centric start-up acquired by Airbnb in 2012. Andrea is a graduate of Oberlin College where she designed her own major, “Technocultural Studies”.
