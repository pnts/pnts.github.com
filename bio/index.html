Andrea Mignolo is a Brooklyn-based designer, speaker, and writer. She is currently the Head of Design & UX at Movable Ink. Previously she was the Chief Design Officer at SimpleReach. She has been a local leader for both the Vancouver and New York branches of the Interaction Design Association, and was the Creative Director of NabeWise, a neighborhood-centric start-up acquired by Airbnb in 2012. Before she got involved in startups she worked on mobile, tablet, and desktop applications for clients including AOL, MoMA, Emily Carr University, Singularity University, The Vancouver Aquarium, and LinkedIn. Andrea is a graduate of Oberlin College where she designed her own major, “Technocultural Studies”.
