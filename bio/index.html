Andrea Mignolo is a Brooklyn-based designer, speaker, and writer. She is currently the Head of Design & UX at Movable Ink and an Executive MBA candidate at the Weatherhead School of Management. Previously she was the Chief Design Officer at SimpleReach, a local leader for both the Vancouver and New York branches of the Interaction Design Association, and the Creative Director of NabeWise, a neighborhood-centric start-up acquired by Airbnb in 2012. Andrea is a graduate of Oberlin College where she designed her own major, “Technocultural Studies”.
