Andrea Mignolo is a full-stack designer living in Brooklyn, New York. Most recently she was the Chief Design Officer at SimpleReach, a startup providing content analytics to the world’s top publishers. She has been a local leader for both the Vancouver and New York branches of the Interaction Design Association, and was the Creative Director of NabeWise, a neighborhood-centric start-up acquired by Airbnb in 2012. She has worked on mobile, tablet, and desktop applications for clients including AOL, MoMA, Emily Carr University, Singularity University, The Vancouver Aquarium, and LinkedIn. Andrea is a graduate of Oberlin College where she designed her own major, “Technocultural Studies”.
